Kaju Resort Farm is a web-based platform designed to enhance the resort’s online presence and streamline its booking operations. The website focuses on delivering a user-friendly experience through intuitive navigation, responsive design, and an efficient online reservation system. By integrating modern web technologies such as PHP and JavaScript, the platform supports dynamic content management, secure booking processes, and effective handling of customer data. Special emphasis is placed on mobile responsiveness and accessibility to ensure a seamless experience across all devices. This project aims to improve customer engagement, simplify reservations, and increase the overall operational efficiency of Kaju Resort Farm.
